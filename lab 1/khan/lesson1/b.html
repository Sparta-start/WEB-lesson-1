Whispers of the Soul

In the silence of the night, a whisper gently weaves, A tale of dreams and hopes, where the heart believes.
Moonlight dances on the waves, a silver, tranquil sea, Reflecting the echoes of the soul, wild and free.
Stars above, like diamonds, sparkle in the velvet sky, Each telling secrets of the universe as they pass by.
Embraced by shadows, the world in peaceful slumber, The whispers of the soul, a melody without a number.
