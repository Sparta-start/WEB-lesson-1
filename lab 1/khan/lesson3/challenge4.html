Recipe #1

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: bru
Ingredients gramm
chiken 300g
macarons 500g
onion 1
shpinat 3
black pepper po vkusu
Salt po vkusu

Step 1: Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan. Add onion and garlic and fry over medium heat until soft, 2-3 minutes.

Step 2: Cut the tomatoes in half. Grate each half using a coarse grater, leaving the skin in your hand.

Step 3: Add pasta. Stir until the sauce is evenly distributed over the pasta.

Step 4: Boil. Then reduce the heat and cook the pasta, covered, until tender, 10-15 minutes, during which time stir several times.

Step 5: You can sprinkle with chopped parsley and serve.

Shashlik

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves: shashlik
Ingredients Quantity
meat 5 kg
Onion 2
water 2l
chiken 3 kg
mionese 200g

Step 1: Place the meat in a bowl (do not use aluminum dishes), add salt and pepper. Add onion. Mix everything well by hand. Marinate the meat for about 60-90 minutes (i.e. while we are grilling and the firewood is burning

Step 2:Thread pieces of pork onto skewers at a short distance from each other, mixed with onions, so that during the cooking process the meat is fried evenly on all sides.

Step 3: Place the skewers on the grill at a minimum distance from the coals. Fry the kebabs on the grill over hot coals, without flame, until cooked, periodically turning the skewers, until golden brown (within 15-20 minutes).
